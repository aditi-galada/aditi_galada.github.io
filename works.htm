Draping (2017)

While making this dress, I first positioned and pinned muslin to develop the structure of the garment after which I removed muslin from the dress form and used it to create the sewing pattern for the dress.

Knotting and Braiding (2017)

The pot hanging is made by using several techniques of knotting and braiding yarns. To make the flowers I used Plaster Of Paris. The stand has a steel base and is covered with yarns.

Mural Painting (2010-2014-2016)

Mural paintings have a facinating feature about them. It seems as though the painting, due to its 3D effect is coming to life. There is something about this that attracts me towards this art. If there is one thing i learnt through this , it is to be patient to create something so beautiful.

Christmas Tree (2016)

This Christmas tree is made completely out of geometric shapes. Not only this, I have not used plastic or any non-biodegradable materials to make this christmas tree, making it eco-friendly.

Miniature Model of a House (2016)

This model is a scaled down version of a house made out of various geometric shapes. The highlight of this model is it's octagonal 8-seater dining table with truncated prism shaped stools that fit into it perfectly.

Craft Documentation of a Handloom Cluster(2017)

I was a part of a research program on the study and analysis of macro and micro environment of weavers in Thirumalpur whose report was submitted to the Textile Ministry of India.

Research Project on E-commerce (2017)

A project report on comparative study of top 5 online apparel shopping websites versus brick & mortar stores. The model consists of unique features of the top 5 sites, modes of payment and advantages of online shopping.
